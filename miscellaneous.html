Corona with Lord Krishna’s Karuna



Why there are Pandemics like Corona ?


1. Law of material world – it’s a temporary place of miseries.
2. To remind us that our real permanent home is not this world but the spiritual world.
3. Punishment by mother nature for various harms caused by men like that of a mother’s punishment to naughty child.
4. Mass Karma – Nature’s way of punishing in mass.


This situation of crisis and fear can be turned into a great opportunity by keeping the right attitude of taking shelter of God as seen in history.


1) Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra – surrendered to Krishna – heard spiritual knowledge – became free from illusions and fears – Bhagavad Gita originated
i. By understanding spiritual knowledge and learning our spiritual duties, we can become free from fear & illusions.
2) King Parikshit – got cursed to die in 7 days – devoted himself in just hearing & remembering about God – Great Srimad Bhagavatam came into existence
i. Most important duty in life is to cultivate remembrance of God
ii. Death – final exam, Test is whether we are able to remember God at that time.
3) Gautama Buddha – brought in royal palace away from all worldly miseries – on visit to kingdom – came across realities of death, old age, diseases – decided to begin spiritual journey to explore about our true identity – realized spiritual dimension of life
i. No material achievements will accompany us after death
ii. Only our spiritual investments – Devotion and service to God – our true wealth which never gets destroyed.
iii. Story of Alexander – asked for 3 things after his death.
▪ Body to be carried by best doctors ( No one can save us except God)
▪ Distribute his wealth ( No one can take one’s riches with himself)
▪ Keep his hands open in grave ( we come empty handed and we leave empty handed)


Important things for application in our lives :


1) Work upon improving the quality of life. What matters is the quality of life not quantity. Many trees are living for thousands of years but their existence has no meaning. Proper quality of life : Life based upon the spiritual principles : Chanting God’s names, following God’s instructions, working for the ultimate aim of life – God realization and going back to Godhead.
2) Take to the real vaccine – which can protect us from all material problems of life for all time to come which are :
a. Chanting of Holy Names of God : Hare Krishna Mahamantra
b. Aligning our life as per God’s desires as expressed in scriptures like Bhagavad Gita, Srimad Bhagavatam etc.
c. Keeping Good company in our life
d. Following A – Association, B – Books , C – Chanting, D – Diet of Prasad, E – service to God
3) Devotee of Lord do not fear death because :
a. He has broad vision of life – Soul is not destroyed – moves to destination based upon previous actions
b. He has full trust in God’s plan - God always takes care of His devotee like a father always cares for his son.
c. Knows that God is always there to take us towards the right destination if we are sincerely trying to live as per God’s desires.
d. A coward dies many times but a brave dies only once.
e. He spends his life in loving and serving God as well serving God’s children – all humanity. E.g :
i. Jesus – sacrificed to spread god’s message – prayed for even the crucifiers.
ii. Swami Prabhupad – in old age travelled to US, preached all over the world – spent whole life spreading spiritual knowledge around the globe


Some positive transformations brought by Corona :


1) Many have stopped animal killing for the sake of meat eating by becoming to vegetarians.
2) Indians have better Job opportunities as many companied are switching from China to India – India emerging as Manufacturing hub.
3) Many people are turning towards spirituality and God to find out answers to questions of life
